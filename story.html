FARM TO CUP

Have you ever wondered, which way does the fragrant cup of coffee in your hand go through? When the waiter brings you a cup of coffee, it is only the end of a long and magical journey: from the selection of the contract land, the screening of the varieties, to the plants, the systems and the collection.

At Wood Stock Farm, we work side by side with each farmer, controlling the standards of the fertilization process; meticulous in the stages of preliminary processing, roasting, brewing... to send customers the best cup of coffee.
